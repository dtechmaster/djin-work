## Our philosophy

And we always ask:

- **Can this solution evolve into something bigger?**
- **Can we generalize it to solve future problems too?**

Well-made solutions become **Blueprints**.
This is how we value our time and create legacies.

**Here, everyone owns their solutions. Love them. Own your work.**

---

## What is a good solution?

A good solution:

- **Solves the real problem**, not the imagined one
- **Is understandable**: others understand how it works
- **Is maintainable**: can be adjusted in the future
- **Has generalization potential**: can become a Blueprint
- **Delivers value**: positively impacts those who use it

---

## How to document your solutions?

Each solution should include:

- **Clear name and description**
- **Problem it solves**
- **How it works** (technical overview)
- **How to use** (practical examples)
- **Known limitations**
- **Evolution potential**: can it become a Blueprint?
